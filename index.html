Featured AI Systems

LuxPilot — Governed LLM Advisory System

LuxPilot is a governed AI advisory system for microenterprises. It separates deterministic routing from probabilistic generation through a three-layer architecture: versioned domain knowledge, an LLM-free rule engine, and model-agnostic LLM execution contracts — so generation happens only under quality gates and human validation.

Designed both as a commercial product and as a research-grade evaluation instrument, with immutable profile snapshots, audit logs, replayable routing decisions, golden-trace fixtures, and evaluation hooks for feasibility, actionability, explainability, and governance integrity.

PartyNestPlan — AI Product for Personalized Children's Parties

PartyNestPlan is a shipped consumer AI product that generates structured, age-aware birthday party plans across educational play frameworks including Montessori, Waldorf-inspired, Reggio Emilia, and play-based approaches.

It demonstrates applied AI product execution: domain modeling, structured generation, personalization logic, user-facing workflows, and monetization around a real consumer need.
