PartyNestPlan — AI Product for Personalized Children's Parties

A shipped consumer AI product that turns a short parent input into structured, age-aware party plans.

What it is

PartyNestPlan is a consumer AI product that generates personalized birthday party plans for children based on how the child plays, the family's constraints, and four educational play frameworks: Montessori, Waldorf-inspired, Reggio Emilia, and play-based approaches.

What I built

I designed the product concept, user flow, personalization logic, offer structure, and AI-generated plan format. The product turns a parent's short input into structured party plans with timelines, activities, host scripts, shopping lists, and printable materials.

Why it matters

PartyNestPlan demonstrates my ability to ship a practical AI product for non-technical users: translating a fuzzy emotional need into structured, usable, paid output. It is evidence of product taste alongside the research-grade systems work in LuxPilot.

Personalization logic & product learnings

  • structured generation works better when the domain schema is explicit;
  • parents need confidence and ready-to-use outputs, not generic inspiration;
  • educational frameworks can guide personalization without making the product feel academic;
  • consumer AI products need clarity, trust, and fast perceived value.
